About TK MUTIARA HARAPAN

TK Mutiara Harapan is a place where every child shines like a pearl — full of curiosity, creativity, and kindness. Located in Tanjung Medan, our kindergarten provides a joyful learning environment that focuses on character, faith, and foundational skills for lifelong learning.

We believe that each child is unique and precious. Through fun, interactive, and meaningful activities, we help them develop not only academic skills but also emotional intelligence, social awareness, and creativity.

Our Vision

“To nurture bright, confident, and faithful children who love to learn and care for others.”

Our Mission

  • Provide joyful and meaningful learning experiences based on love and respect.
  • Develop children’s creativity, curiosity, and confidence.
  • Encourage faith, discipline, and good moral values in every activity.
  • Build strong collaboration between teachers, parents, and the community.

Our Learning Approach

We implement the Scientific and Play-Based Learning Approach that encourages exploration, questioning, and discovery. Every lesson integrates literacy, numeracy, and character education through songs, games, and storytelling.

Facilities

  • Colorful and safe classrooms
  • Creative learning corners
  • Outdoor play area and garden
  • Multimedia learning tools
  • Clean and child-friendly environment

Meet Our Principal

Yulia Permal br. Sihombing, M.Pd leads TK Mutiara Harapan with warmth and dedication. With years of experience in early childhood education, she believes that teaching is not just about knowledge, but about touching hearts and inspiring futures.
